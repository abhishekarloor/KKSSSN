About Kishkindha Karnataka

Kishkindha Karnataka Souharda Sahakari Sangha Niyamitha is a registered cooperative society established in 2015, dedicated to providing comprehensive financial services to the people of Gangavathi and surrounding areas. With registration number 34567 from the Registrar of Co-Operative Societies, Government of Karnataka, we operate on the core principles of cooperation, trust, and customer service.

Our mission is to serve the financial needs of lower, middle, and upper-middle-class communities through affordable loans, secure savings schemes, and modern Financial facilities. We combine traditional cooperative values with contemporary Financial technology to deliver excellence.
