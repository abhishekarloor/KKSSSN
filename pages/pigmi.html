Pigmy Account

Build savings through regular small deposits with attractive returns

What is a Pigmy Account?

Pigmy Account is a micro-savings scheme designed for individuals and business owners who want to save small amounts regularly. It's an ideal savings vehicle that allows you to build wealth through consistent deposits. Our bank staff will visit your home or workplace to collect deposits, making saving convenient and hassle-free. All deposits are insured and you can access loans against your Pigmy Account balance.

Interest Rates

Interest Rate Structure

  • First 6 Months: 3% interest (if balance maintained throughout period)
  • After 6 Months: Up to 5% annual interest per year

Interest Calculation: Interest is calculated daily on the basis of per year percentage and credited periodically to your account.

*Interest rates subject to change as per bank policy and RBI guidelines

Eligibility & Documents

Eligibility Criteria

  • ✓ Indian citizen
  • ✓ Individual or business owner
  • ✓ Must be 18+ years of age
  • ✓ Existing bank customer (optional)

Required Documents

  • • Passport-size photograph
  • • Address proof (Aadhar card, electricity bill, telephone bill)
  • • Pan card (Xerox copy)
  • • Business proof (if applicable)

Note: If you already have a savings or current account with KKSSSN, you can open a Pigmy Account without providing additional documents.

How to Open a Pigmy Account

  1. Visit Our Branch: Come to KKSSSN branch with your required documents
  2. Complete Application Form: Fill the Pigmy Account opening form with your details
  3. Document Verification: Our staff will verify your documents and complete KYC
  4. Make First Deposit: Deposit minimum ₹50 and receive your passbook
  5. Schedule Collection: Decide on weekly, fortnightly, or monthly collection schedule
  6. Start Saving: Our representatives will collect deposits from your home/business as per schedule
