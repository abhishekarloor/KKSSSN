What is a Fixed Deposit?

A Fixed Deposit (FD) is a financial instrument that allows you to invest a lump sum amount for a fixed tenure at a predetermined interest rate. It is one of the safest investment options that provides guaranteed returns upon maturity. Unlike savings accounts, FDs offer higher interest rates and are ideal for individuals looking for stable, predictable income without market risks.

Eligibility & Documents

Eligibility Criteria

  • ✓ Indian citizen
  • ✓ Minimum 18+ years of age
  • ✓ Individual or Joint deposit facility
  • ✓ Parents can open for minors (18 years and below)

Required Documents

  • • Two passport-size photographs
  • • Aadhar Card
  • • PAN Card (Xerox)
  • • Voter ID or driving license
  • • Ration card or utility bill

Note: We require any three official documents from the above list as proof of identity and address. For minors, guardianship documents are required.

How to Open a Fixed Deposit

  1. Visit Our Branch: Come to KKSSSN with your documents and ID proof
  2. Choose FD Parameters: Decide on deposit amount, tenure, and interest payout frequency
  3. Complete Application: Fill FD opening form with your financial details
  4. Document Verification: Our staff will verify all your documents
  5. Make Deposit: Pay the FD amount (minimum ₹500)
  6. Receive FD Certificate: Get your FD certificate and passbook with maturity details
