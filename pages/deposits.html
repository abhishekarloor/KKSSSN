Fixed Deposits (FD)

Guaranteed returns with flexible maturity options

Why Choose FD?

  • ✓ Guaranteed Returns: Fixed interest rate for the tenure
  • ✓ Flexible Tenure: From 7 days to 5 years
  • ✓ Multiple Options: Monthly, quarterly, half-yearly, annual interest
  • ✓ Loan Against FD: Up to 90% of FD amount
  • ✓ Safe & Secure: Your principal is safe and insured
