Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our banking services

Account Related

What documents are needed to open a savings account?

You need a valid ID proof (Aadhar/PAN/Passport), address proof (utility bill/passport/Aadhar), and a recent photograph. Visit our branch and we'll guide you through the process.

What is the minimum balance requirement?

Our savings account has a low minimum balance requirement. Please visit our branch or call us for current minimum balance details.

Can I open an account jointly?

Yes, you can open joint accounts with family members. Both applicants must visit our branch with required documents for joint account opening.

Loan Related

What is the interest rate for gold loans?

Our gold loan interest rates start from 11% p.a. The exact rate depends on loan amount, tenure, and LTV ratio. Visit our branch for personalized quotes.

How long does loan approval take?

Most loans are approved within 2-3 working days after document submission. Gold loans can be processed within 24 hours for eligible applicants.

What documents are needed for a home loan?

You'll need ID proof, address proof, income documents (salary slips/IT returns), property documents, and bank statements. Our loan officers will provide a complete checklist.

Can I prepay my loan without penalty?

Yes, partial prepayment is allowed without any charges. Full prepayment may have minimal charges. Contact our branch for specific terms.

Deposits Related

What is the minimum amount for FD?

The minimum FD amount is ₹1,000. There is no maximum limit - you can deposit as much as you want.

Can I withdraw FD before maturity?

Yes, premature withdrawal is allowed. However, you'll receive a lower interest rate compared to the full tenure rate. Reduced interest will be calculated as per our policy.

Are deposits insured or protected?

Yes, all deposits are protected under the DICGC (Deposit Insurance and Credit Guarantee Corporation) scheme up to ₹5,00,000 per depositor per bank.

What is the RD minimum deposit amount?

The minimum monthly RD amount is ₹100. You can increase this as per your convenience.

Services Related

Is SMS alert service available?

Yes, SMS alert service is completely FREE. You'll receive instant notifications for every transaction on your account. Enable it at our branch or call us.

Can I make UPI payments through my account?

Yes, UPI services are available. You can make instant, safe payments 24/7 with zero transaction charges using UPI at our bank.

How much does a locker facility cost?

Locker rental varies based on size. Charges range from ₹500 to ₹2,000 per annum. Visit our branch to see available sizes and book a locker.

What is NEFT and how does it work?

NEFT (National Electronic Funds Transfer) is a payment system for transferring money across India. Transfers are settled by next working day. Charges apply as per our service charges.

General

What are your working hours?

We are open Monday to Saturday from 10:00 AM to 5:00 PM. We remain closed on Sundays and government holidays.

How do I file a complaint or grievance?

You can file a complaint in person at our branch, by phone (+91 80738-22387), by email (kksssn@gmail.com), or by mail. We have a dedicated Grievance Redressal Officer.

Is KKSSSN regulated by the RBI?

We are registered with the Registrar of Co-Operative Societies, Government of Karnataka and operate under RBI guidelines. We follow all compliance and transparency norms.

Can senior citizens get higher interest rates?

Yes! Senior citizens (60+ years) get an additional 0.5% interest on FD and RD schemes. Please visit our branch with age proof.

Didn't find your answer?

Contact us directly - our team is always ready to help!
