About Kishkindha Karnataka

Building Trust, Creating Prosperity, Serving Our Community

Our History

Kishkindha Karnataka Souharda Sahakari Sangha Niyamitha was established in 2025 with the vision of providing accessible, transparent, and customer-centric financial services to the people of Gangavathi and surrounding areas.

With Registration Number: 34567 from the Registrar of Co-Operative Societies, Government of Karnataka, we began our operations with a core belief that banking should serve everyone, regardless of their financial status.

Though newly established, we are committed to building a trusted financial institution serving our community with integrity, transparency, and genuine care for our customers' financial well-being. We are leveraging modern technology and best practices from day one to provide world-class banking services.

Milestones & Achievements

  • 2025 (Foundation Year): Kishkindha Karnataka Souharda Sahakari Sangha Niyamitha officially established with registration
  • 2025 (Q1): Launched in Gangavathi with initial team and infrastructure setup
  • 2025 (Q2): Introduced 9 comprehensive loan products (Gold, Home, Vehicle, Business, Education, Mahila Udyog, Mortgage, Unsecured, Overdraft)
  • 2025 (Q2): Launched savings accounts, pigmy deposits, and fixed deposit schemes
  • 2025 (Q3): Implemented digital banking services - Online Transfers, UPI, NEFT, and Mobile Banking
  • 2025 (Q3): Added advanced tools including EMI Calculator, SMS Alert Service, and Online Services Platform
  • 2025 (Q4): Launched Careers & Recruitment Program, Gallery section, and FAQ support center
  • 2026 & Beyond: Expanding member base, community engagement, and continuous innovation in financial services

Vision & Mission

🎯 Our Mission

Why Cooperative Banking?

The advantages of banking with a cooperative society

💼 Community-Focused

Cooperative banks prioritize community welfare and member interests over profits. We reinvest earnings back into our community.

🤝 Member Ownership

As a member, you're a part owner of KKSSSN. You have a voice in decisions through our democratic governance structure.

💰 Better Rates

We offer competitive interest rates on deposits and loans, with lower margins and better terms than traditional banks.

📱 Personal Service

Our staff knows our members personally and can provide customized solutions for your specific financial needs.

🌱 Sustainable Growth

We focus on sustainable growth that benefits the entire community, not just shareholders.

🏆 Highest Ethical Standards

Regulated by the Registrar of Co-Operative Societies and follow RBI guidelines for ethical banking practices.
